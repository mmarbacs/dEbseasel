Hello and Welcome!

Thank you for checking out my site!

"Art has always been a passion of mine. I can recall, as a young child, looking for objects around the house to sketch and during high school, spending time on weekends painting murals. I grew up, life got busy and creativity was put on the back burner. A very sudden health scare stopped me abruptly in my tracks and the first place that I turned to was art. Art brought me peace and solace during a very challenging time, and for that, I am grateful.

The medium I use is mostly acrylics and I am drawn to nature. Playing with colour, texture and shape, using different tools to manipulate paint and allowing the paint to flow and come to life is what brings me joy.

My hope is to pass on my art to family, friends and beyond. I look forward to a future where I will continue my lifelong, creative journey."

Deb Peterson
